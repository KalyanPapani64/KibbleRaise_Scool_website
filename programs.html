Programs

Here is our academic cirriculum. Our annual subject calander is different for different grades/classes. So, Here is our total annual program view.

pre-kindergarten

pre-kindergarten/Nursury focus on establishing a parent-free environment. our teachers establish a level of comfort with the children enabling them to adapt to their peers and surroundings. Learning through play is the main approach used and the introduction of basics of literacy, phonics, and story sessions are a key component of the program.

LKG

Lower Kindergarten (LKG) focuses on building writing and reading skills, number concepts, and science through experiments. Interesting and thought-provoking activities including field trips. learn lower and upper-case letters. Numeracy is integrated using numbers, shapes and concepts.

UKG

Upper Kindergarten (UKG) focus on strengthens the writing and reading skills of the students. After assessing the childrens prior knowledge, we work with them to improve their potential through mental mathematics

class 1

Learners are now guided further through the subject areas of Language, Mathematics, Science & Social Studies. Physical movement, music and dance are an integral part of the curriculum.

class 2

This program views the child as an explorer and an inquirer making them independent learners with enhanced listening, thinking and researching skills. Learners inherent skills through conceptual learning in Numeracy, Literacy, Science, Social Studies.Physical Education, Performing Arts and Visual Arts form an important part of the curriculum.

class 3

Students of Grade III will focus on enhancing and strengthening strategies like Comprehension, Listening, Reading etc. Learners become intense in application of rules of grammar. They start keenly observing ways to solve Mathematical problems. Integrated through with an additional languages Hindi and English. Their interests and creativity are streamlined in Visual Arts and Performing Arts as well as Physical Education.

class 4

Our students understand the interconnectedness of subject areas through exploring concepts, ideas, and issues of local significance. They acquire in-depth knowledge and develop understanding across a broad and balanced range of disciplines. Students gain confidence in both spoken and written additional language.

class 5

We encourage our students to understand, appreciate their own cultures and histories and to be open to the perspectives, values, and traditions of other individuals and communities. We encourage them to approach uncertainty with courage and forethought and have the independence of spirit to explore new roles, ideas, and strategies.The urge to question is enhanced.
